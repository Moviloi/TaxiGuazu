What's included in the transfer

Everything you need
for your day at the Falls

We pick you up at your hotel

We depart from your accommodation in Puerto Iguazú. The park is about 20 minutes away by car — we coordinate the departure time based on when you want to enter.

Waiting included

We drop you off at the park entrance and wait until you finish. A full tour takes 4 to 6 hours — we coordinate the waiting time before departure.

Return included

When you're done, we take you back to your hotel or other destination in Puerto Iguazú. No need to look for transportation at the park exit.

Guidance included

We tell you which circuits to take, how the train works, when to avoid crowds, and the best time to see Devil's Throat.

Park entrance fee

The Iguazu National Park entrance fee is paid separately at the ticket office. We inform you of the current price and whether it's better to buy it online in advance.

Group larger than 4?

We coordinate multiple vehicles. Contact us in advance for a custom quote.

What you'll find
in the park

  1. 1
    Devil's Throat

    The most impressive waterfall. Access via train and walkway. We recommend going early to avoid crowds.

  2. 2
    Upper Circuit

    Panoramic views from above the falls. Less crowded. Great for photos.

  3. 3
    Lower Circuit

    At water level. You'll get wet — bring a change of clothes or a raincoat. The most spectacular up close.

  4. 4
    Jungle Train

    Travels through the park between stations. Included with entrance fee. Stops at Falls and Devil's Throat.
