Assisted AR–PY crossing

Friendship Bridge, Argentine and Paraguayan customs. We guide you at every step. With proper documentation, the crossing takes 20 to 40 minutes depending on the day.

Waiting while you shop

We drop you off at the shopping area and wait. We coordinate the waiting time before departure — typically 3 to 5 hours depending on what you plan to buy.

What to buy

Electronics (laptops, cameras, cell phones), perfumes and cosmetics, sports shoes, brand clothing, musical instruments, and home goods. Prices well below Argentina.

Currency and purchase limit

In Paraguay you use Guaraníes or US Dollars. The tax-free purchase limit for Argentines is USD 300 per person. We inform you of the current allowance before the trip.

Basic guidance

We tell you which shopping areas are most convenient, what to avoid, how to declare at customs on return, and how to handle shopping luggage in the car.

Group larger than 4?

We coordinate multiple vehicles for groups. Contact us in advance for a custom quote.

What you need
to cross to Paraguay

  1. 1
    Documentation

    Valid ID for Argentines. Passport for foreigners. No upcoming expiration date.

  2. 2
    Money

    Guaraníes or US Dollars for purchases. In Ciudad del Este there are exchange houses and ATMs.

  3. 3
    Purchase limit

    USD 300 per person tax-free for Argentines. Declare everything at customs on return.

  4. 4
    Recommended schedule

    Leave early (before 9am) to cross before peak hour. Avoid Fridays and weekends.
